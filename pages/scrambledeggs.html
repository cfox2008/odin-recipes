Scrambled Egg Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp milk
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs and place in a bowl
  2. Add milk and salt and pepper to bowl and whisk until eggs are beaten
  3. Add olive oil to skillet over medium heat. When oil is hot, add eggs to skillet.
  4. Stir while cooking until eggs reach desired consitency.
