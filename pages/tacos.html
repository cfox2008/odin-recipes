Taco Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for crunchy tacos made with ground turkey and black beans!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 box of taco shells
  • 1 bag of shredded lettuce
  • 3 tbsp of taco seasoning
  • 1 bag of monterey jack cheese
  • 3/4 cup of water

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, brown the ground turkey over medium-high heat.
  2. Drain grease from skillet, then add black beans, taco seasoning and water to skillet.
  3. Simmer over medium-high heat until all liquid is absorbed.
  4. Add taco meat to taco shells. Top with cheese and lettuce.
